Available Commands

  • /play - Start playing the game
  • /help - List all commands
  • /mine - Start a 2 hour mining expedition
  • /launch - Launch a rocket
  • /colony-create - Create a colony on your current planet
  • /colony-upgrade - Upgrade your colony
  • /colony - View colony stats
  • /nova - Check your Nova Credits (In game currency)
  • /rocket-upgrade - Upgrade your rocket
  • /rocket - View your current rockets stats
  • /location - View what planet you're on
  • /store - Shop for a new rocket
  • /trade - Trade earned resources for Nova Credits
